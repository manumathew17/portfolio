EasyEcom Warehouse Management App

  • Category: App
  • Technology worked: Native Android, Java, Kotlin
  • Project URL: play store

Info

As the architect and leader behind the Warehouse Management App, I have spearheaded the development from the ground up, orchestrating a seamless system for essential ground movements in warehouse operations. The app is a cornerstone for warehouse keepers, offering unprecedented ease and efficiency in their daily tasks.

Key Functionalities:

  • Goods Receipt Note (GRN): Facilitates the streamlined recording of incoming goods, ensuring accuracy and accountability.
  • Put Away: Optimizes the allocation of received goods to their designated storage locations, enhancing warehouse organization.
  • Picklist: Enables efficient order picking by generating organized lists for warehouse staff, minimizing errors and delays.
  • Order Confirmation: Streamlines the confirmation process, providing a systematic approach to order fulfillment.

Innovative Features:

The Warehouse Management App incorporates cutting-edge technology to elevate its functionality and productivity:

  • Barcode Scanning Library: Through dedicated research and development, I have implemented a robust barcode scanning library. This feature ensures accurate and swift data input, reducing manual errors.
  • External USB Barcode Scanner Integration: Leveraging the power of USB On-The-Go (OTG), the app seamlessly connects with external USB barcode scanners. This integration has proven to increase overall productivity by an impressive 70%.

Team Leadership:

I have successfully managed a dedicated team throughout the app's development, fostering collaboration and innovation. Our collective efforts have resulted in a Warehouse Management App that not only meets but exceeds the expectations of warehouse keepers, enhancing their operational efficiency.

Future Developments:

The journey doesn't end here. I am committed to continuous improvement, exploring opportunities for further optimization and innovation in warehouse management technology.
